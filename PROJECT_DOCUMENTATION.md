## ðŸ“Œ 5. Methodology

The following analytical steps were performed using SQL:

### 5.1 Funnel Analysis

### 5.2 Traffic Source Analysis

Evaluated performance of marketing channels such as Organic, Direct, Referral, and Paid Search based on conversion rate and revenue generated.

---

### 5.3 Device-Based Conversion Analysis

Compared conversion rates across:

* Mobile
* Desktop
* Tablet

---

### 5.4 Cohort Retention Analysis

Grouped users based on first visit month and tracked their return behaviour in subsequent months to measure retention rate.

---

### 5.5 Customer Lifetime Value (LTV) Analysis

Calculated total revenue generated by each user and segmented users into deciles using NTILE() window function.

---

### 5.6 Churn Analysis

Identified users who visited the platform but did not return within 7 days using LEAD() window function and DATE_DIFF().

---

### 5.7 A/B Testing Simulation

Simulated an experiment by segmenting users into:

* Group A (Mobile Users)
* Group B (Desktop Users)

Compared conversion rates and average revenue between groups.

## ðŸ“Œ 6. Key Business Insights

* Significant drop-offs were observed at the add-to-cart stage of the conversion funnel.
* Desktop users demonstrated a higher conversion rate compared to mobile users.
* Organic traffic contributed the highest number of users, while paid traffic generated higher revenue per user.
* A majority of users churned within the first week of visit.
* Top 10% of users contributed a significant portion of total revenue.
